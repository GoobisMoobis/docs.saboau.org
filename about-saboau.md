# About SaboAU
Sabotaged Among Us Modding, also known as SabotagedAU, Sabotaged AU Modding, or simply SaboAU, is a modding organization currently ran by [GoobisMoobis](https://github.com/GoobisMoobis) (Goober), [TheGlitch13](https://github.com/TheGlitch13) (Glitch), and [WashedBac](https://github.com/washedbac) (Washed). It was originally founded by [ninja-au](https://github.com/ninja-au) (Ninja) under the name Ninja Among Us Modding, as a way to work on [MiraModule]({{ '/miraoverloaded/history-of-miraoverloaded/' | relative_url }}) with Goober.

{: .note }
> MiraModule is no longer called MiraModule! When the name of Ninja AU Modding was changed to Sabotaged AU Modding, the name of it was changed to [Welcome to the **MiraOverloaded** wiki!]({{ '/miraoverloaded/' | relative_url }}).

Eventually, Glitch and Washed were invited to join the organization to work on MiraModule.

Early April 2026, after concerns were raised with Ninja's conduct within the Mira community, the team decided to develop MiraModule under a new GitHub organization, which would later be named Sabotaged Among Us Modding (SaboAU).
Ninja has since been welcomed back as a contributor for our projects after relations between him and the team reverted to their pre-drama levels.
