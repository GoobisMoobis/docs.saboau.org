{: .warning }
> This guide assumes you have already installed [Town of Us: Mira](https://github.com/AU-Avengers/TOU-Mira)

{: .note }
> This guide assumes you have already downloaded the [MiraOverloaded DLL](https://github.com/SabotagedAU/MiraOverloaded/releases). If you are having trouble downloading the DLL, join our [Discord](https://discord.saboau.org) for support.

## Steam
1. Right-click Among Us in your steam library.
2. Click `Manage` > `Browse Local Files`
3. In File Explorer, navigate to your `Common` folder. You can find this folder by looking at the top of File Explorer. Click the red square in the image below: ![common-folder-nav]({{ '/assets/common-folder-nav.png' | relative_url }})
4. Locate your Among Us install that includes Town of Us Mira.
5. Once inside of your TOU install, navigate to `BepInEx` > `Plugins`. You should see your mod files, including Mira itself, and various bundles.
6. Move `MiraOverloaded.dll` into this folder.
7. Relaunch the game and verify that the **Sabotaged Among Us Modding** logo shows up on the loading screen. If it doesn't, join our [Discord](https://discord.saboau.org) for support.

## Epic Games
{: .warning }
> Make sure that you have not only installed [Town of Us: Mira](https://github.com/AU-Avengers/TOU-Mira), but also [EpicGamesStarter](https://github.com/whichtwix/EpicGamesStarter/releases/tag/1.1.0).
> Verify that it works and that you can launch base TOU Mira.
> You do not need this if you are using an alternate Epic launcher such as Heroic or Legendary.

1. Make sure you've disabled auto-updating on Among Us. You can do this by uninstalling Among Us from your Epic library and during re-install uncheck the auto update option.
2. Navigate to wherever you have Among Us installed. This will be different for everyone due to EpicGamesStarter/Heroic.
3. Ensure that your install has TOU installed.
4. Once inside of your TOU install, navigate to `BepInEx` > `Plugins`. You should see your mod files, including Mira itself, and various bundles.
5. Move `MiraOverloaded.dll` into this folder.
6. Relaunch the game and verify that the **Sabotaged Among Us Modding** logo shows up on the loading screen. If it doesn't, join our [Discord](https://discord.saboau.org) for support. We do get the most problems with Epic users, unfortunately. You might also want to join the [Town Of Us Discord Server](https://discord.com/invite/town-of-us) if you're having general install troubles.

## Microsoft Store/Xbox App
1. Open the Xbox App, right click Among Us and click `Manage`
2. Go to the **Files** tab > `Browse...`
3. Go to the `Content` folder within the **Among Us** folder. Make sure you've already installed [Town of Us: Mira](https://github.com/AU-Avengers/TOU-Mira) inside this folder.
4. Once inside of your TOU install, navigate to `BepInEx` > `Plugins`. You should see your mod files, including Mira itself, and various bundles.
5. Move `MiraOverloaded.dll` into this folder.
6. Relaunch the game and verify that the **Sabotaged Among Us Modding** logo shows up on the loading screen. If it doesn't, join our [Discord](https://discord.saboau.org) for support.

## Itch.io
1. In your Itch.io library, right-click Among Us, then click `Manage` > `Open folder in explorer`
2. Find the Among Us folder that you have installed [Town of Us: Mira](https://github.com/AU-Avengers/TOU-Mira) to.
3. Once inside of your TOU install, navigate to `BepInEx` > `Plugins`. You should see your mod files, including Mira itself, and various bundles.
4. Move `MiraOverloaded.dll` into this folder.
5. Relaunch the game and verify that the **Sabotaged Among Us Modding** logo shows up on the loading screen. If it doesn't, join our [Discord](https://discord.saboau.org) for support.
## Android
MiraOverloaded is not available on [Starlight](https://allofus.dev/starlight/) which is currently the only mod launcher for Android devices. Support for Starlight is planned eventually.

## IOS, Console
Neither of these platforms are supported by [Town of Us: Mira](https://github.com/AU-Avengers/TOU-Mira) and therefore are not supported by MiraOverloaded.

## Linux/MacOS
There are various ways to get mods on both of these platforms. join the [Town Of Us Discord Server](https://discord.com/invite/town-of-us) and look for the channel labeled [`#🌎│mira-information`](https://discord.com/channels/890249154402586734/1377177841967566949) to find both more detailed install guides for Mira, and also general install guides for Linux and MacOS.
